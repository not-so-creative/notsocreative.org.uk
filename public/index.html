Who are we?

We’re a couple of computer science students from the University of Kent with a keen interest in productivity and open source. Its due to our shared interests that we got together to work a few different different over the past few years. Working under the banner of “Not So Creative” we hope to create tools/code that helps improve not just our own productivity.

Creations

Eisenhower

What is important is seldom urgent and what is urgent is seldom important. Dwight D. Eisenhower

Productivity is a key player in feeling good, and this tool aims to improve your productivity by plotting collections of tasks based on their percieved urgency and importance.

Inspired by Eisenhower Decision Matricies and the BulletJournalling systems for task management and priortization this tool allows for a very visual and effective way to get things done in a more effective manner.
